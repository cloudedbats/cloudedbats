About the test

The diagram shows bat activities during 5 minutes (300 sec.). Peak frequencies are shown for each millisecond, and the size reflects the amplitude. Use the tool bar above the diagram to zoom and navigate in the diagram. Peak values are shown when hovering over the small circles.

I'm also working on other metrics suitable for visualisation in other time-scales like hour, night, week, month and season.

Data sources for the diagram:
- Sonic visualiser image of the original wav-file.
- Original wav-file (230 MB). 384 kHz at 16 bits during 5 minutes.
- File with peak frequencies (37 kB).
